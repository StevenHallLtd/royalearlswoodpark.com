Intro

Royal Earlswood Park is a luxury housing estate located in Earlswood, Surrey.

Situated just South of London, the development has great links to the Capital and beyond, with direct trains from Earlswood station a 10 minute walk away and the M25 and Gatwick Airport just 10 minutes by car. A bus also operates on site.

The site incorporates over 130 dwellings to suit a variety of budgets and needs, built in and around the Victorian buildings of this former asylum, which was fully stripped out and converted between 1997 and 2002, with new housing added.

Still boasting many period features, with a grand entrance hall and impressive swimming pool and gym surrounded by acres of manicured grounds and endless country walks, Royal Earlswood Park offers a truly unique living experience.
